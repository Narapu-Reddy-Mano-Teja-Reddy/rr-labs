Panel Systems

Cleanroom Panel Types

Choose from our full range of industry-certified wall and ceiling panel solutions — each engineered for airtight performance, cGMP compliance, and long-term durability.

Powder Coated Panels

Electrostatically applied epoxy-polyester finish over mild steel. Provides exceptional corrosion resistance, smooth flush surfaces, and a wide colour palette — ideal for ISO Class 7 & 8 environments.

ISO Class 7–8

PPGI Panels

Pre-Painted Galvanised Iron panels with factory-applied polymer coating on zinc-coated steel. Delivers superior moisture and chemical resistance with high structural rigidity — suited for humid or temperature-sensitive rooms.

Moisture Resistant

PUF Panels

Polyurethane Foam core panels sandwiched between metal skins. Outstanding thermal insulation (low U-value), lightweight construction, and excellent sound attenuation — perfect for controlled-temperature cleanrooms and cold storage labs.

Thermal Insulation

Relay Panels

Rock-wool or aluminium-laminate core relay panels engineered for fire-resistance and enhanced acoustic performance. Their interlocking cam-lock joints ensure rapid assembly with zero-gap sealing — widely specified for pharmaceutical and biotech facilities.

Fire Resistant

SS Panels

Grade 304 / 316 Stainless Steel panels with electropolished or satin finish. Completely non-porous, non-particle-shedding, and resistant to aggressive cleaning agents — the gold standard for ISO Class 4–6 and sterile manufacturing environments.

ISO Class 4–6
